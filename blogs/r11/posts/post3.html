1/4/16 Weekly Recap

This article is about what Room 11 has been doing from 30/3/16 - 1/4/16.

Posted by Emily Dean and Cameron Dickie on April 1, 2016

Poems

We have been analysing poems from Robert Frost and Jim Wayne Miller. It helped us use vocabulary in the right places, have a good structure when writing poems and use new techniques for poems and just writing in general. We also wrote our own poems about change, although some people did poems with their own topics.

Maori (No accents in HTML)

At school we have been learning Te Reo Maori. We have learnt basic three word sentences in Maori. An example of these sentences are;

Who are you?
